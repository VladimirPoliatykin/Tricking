Like Tricking?

So you're not wrong

Tricking (English tricking, martial arts tricking.) - The sports movement, which is a combination of movements from martial arts, gymnastics, breakdancing and other sports disciplines to create a spectacular mix of rotations in different planes, screws and kicks. The word "tricking" - the common abbreviation for the full name of discipline - martial arts tricking, in Russian meaning "martial arts tricks".

Unlike many sports disciplines, tricking has no rules and systems, as there is no regulatory framework that governs the sport. Strictly speaking, the participants are free to perform any dynamic movement in the plane and call it a "trick" - although there are certain movements that are generally accepted in the tricking.
